About Me

Welcome to my personal Portfolio. I am a data Scientist with passion for data-based discoveries, programming and football. Ever ssince I was a kid, I loved understanding how various devices functioned and I liked to press any button on any device I saw in order to see the functioal effect on the device. In my first ever coding project, I had to write a python programme that will capture bytes, serially transmitted from a pulse oximeter module, decode them and print the results to a monitor. This programme ran on a raspberry pi. I also implemented functionanilities to enable various interactions between the pulse oximeter and a user. Learning how to programme helped me fulfill my dreams as I was not able to write code that affected the behaviour of a device. I subsequently combined my newly learned skill with my passion for statistics to go into data science.
